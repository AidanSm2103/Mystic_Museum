About Mystic Museum

The Mystic Museum is dedicated to preserving and showcasing the rich history and culture of mysticism and the supernatural. Our exhibits feature artifacts of great power and importance, some of which could threaten the balance of this world should they fall into the wrong hands...

Our Founder

Our founder, Dr. Archie Lockheart was a renowned historian, mystic and occultist. Dr. Lockheart has spent his life uncovering the secrets that lie beyond the veil. He tragically passed away in 1969 under mysterious circumstances, police ruled suicide but many believe there was more to the story.

Our Philosophy

Preserve those that came before us. Protect those that come after us.

Runes

Our artifacts contain great power, for this reason they have been placed under a protective spell. Those with a keen eye may find the correct runes to lift the spell and behold our great artifacts.
